2. Design Alternatives

❌ Option A: Pure Social Matching Mode

Description: Match runners nearby with the same pace based on location.

Reasons for Rejection:

  • On-site environment is complex, runners may be unwilling to match strangers due to safety/social anxiety
  • Lack of fun, lacking clear task objectives
  • Low matching success rate, unstable user experience

✅ Option B (Final Choice): LBS Geofencing Treasure Hunt System

Description: Divide the map into virtual task points, trigger wearable device feedback when reaching designated locations.

Selection Reasons:

  • Perfectly matches "Wearable, Location-based" platform characteristics
  • Strong gamification motivation for runners
  • Organizers (PC/Mobile) can flexibly set up on-site activities
